Sponsors

With over 5 million monthly visitors worldwide and roughly 4 million Facebook fans, stylefruits is the leading social shopping platform for fashion and interior design in Europe. Headquartered in Munich, Germany, stylefruits GmbH combines online shopping with innovative social media features.

The stylefruits website offers a wide range of interior design and fashion products by featuring items from online shops in the fashion and home segments. Thanks to intuitive design tools, stylefruits users can easily combine thousands of products to create trendy outfits and living styles, and then post these creations on the stylefruits website, Facebook, or other social media sites so that they can be rated and discussed before making a purchase.

Founded in 2008 by Ingo Heinrich, Michael Vietze, and Mathias Ziegler, stylefruits GmbH currently employs an international team of around 80 coworkers with operations in Germany, the UK, France, and the Netherlands.
